About Our School

Founded on the principles of Maria Montessori, Elyon Montessori School offers an environment where children lead their own learning journey. We do not just teach; we inspire.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating Montessori environment that guides children to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in early childhood education, fostering future innovators and leaders.

Our Philosophy

At Elyon Montessori, we believe every child is unique and capable. We create a learning space that encourages discovery, independence, confidence, and creativity.

Our Core Values

  • Holistic humanity
  • Self discipline
  • Goal-oriented
  • Diligence and hard work
  • Excellence

Our Programs

Age-appropriate, hands-on Montessori programs designed to ignite curiosity and love for learning.

Crèche & Nursery

Ages 6 months – 2 years. Nurturing environment focused on sensory exploration and early bonding.

KG1 & KG2

Ages 3 – 5. Montessori materials, practical life, early reading, and numeracy skills.

Primary (Basic 1–6)

Ages 6 – 12. Full academic curriculum with STEM, arts, and character development.

JHS (Junior High)

Ages 12 – 15. Focused on BECE preparation, advanced science, and leadership skills.

Why Choose Elyon Montessori?

We go above and beyond to ensure every child thrives.

Small Class Sizes

Our low student-to-teacher ratio ensures personalised attention for every learner.

Digital Integration

Online portals for parents and teachers. Real-time academic tracking and communication.

Holistic Growth

Beyond academics — swimming, arts, music, sports, and moral education.

Safe & Secure

CCTV-monitored premises. Background-checked staff. A place where your child is always safe.
